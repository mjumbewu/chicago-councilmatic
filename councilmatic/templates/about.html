What is This?

Councilmatic Chicago lets you know what the Chicago city council is doing. If you care about changes in zoning, what the city is paying out in settlements, or BIRTHDAYS!!!, you can find out here. You can even subscribe to this search, and if something new happens that you care about you can get an e-mail or see it in your RSS reader.

You can also see and follow all the legislation sponsored by your alderman or mayor (Bob Fioretti, Leslie Hairston, Rahm Emanuel).

What Else?

If there is a piece of legislation you care about, you can give your opinion on it, share it with your network, and argue about it with other folks

About the Data

The data on this site is scraped once a week from from the Chicago City Council legislation site. Councilmatic may not have up-to-the-minute information about the legislation you are viewing, since it scrapes once per week. Each piece of legislation provides a link to its source for reference.
